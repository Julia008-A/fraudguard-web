Key design decisions
Why PR-AUC not accuracy?
With 0.17% fraud rate, predicting all-legitimate achieves 99.83% accuracy and catches zero fraud. PR-AUC evaluates only the positive class.
Why threshold 0.74 not 0.5?
Default 0.5 is calibrated for 50% base rates. With 0.17% fraud, high confidence is required. 0.74 maximises F1 on the precision-recall curve.
Why SMOTE inside CV folds?
Applying SMOTE before splitting leaks synthetic points from validation data into training. ImbPipeline enforces correct fold-level application.
